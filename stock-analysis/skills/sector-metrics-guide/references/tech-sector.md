# Technology Sector Metrics

## SaaS / Cloud Software

**Representative Companies:** CRM, NOW, SNOW, DDOG, ZS, CRWD

| Metric | Description | Good Level |
|--------|-------------|------------|
| **ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue)** | Annual recurring revenue | 30%+ growth excellent |
| **NRR (Net Revenue Retention)** | Existing customer revenue retention | 120%+ excellent |
| **Gross Retention** | Revenue retained without churn | 90%+ healthy |
| **Rule of 40** | Revenue growth % + Operating margin % | 40%+ healthy |
| **CAC Payback** | Customer acquisition cost recovery period | 12 months or less |
| **LTV/CAC** | Lifetime value / Acquisition cost | 3x+ excellent |
| **Gross Margin** | Gross profit margin | 70%+ SaaS standard |
| **FCF Margin** | Free cash flow margin | 20%+ excellent |

### Key Notes
- Many companies are unprofitable, so **ARR growth, Rule of 40** are key
- GAAP losses acceptable if FCF positive
- NRR above 100% means expansion within existing customers

### Valuation Considerations
- Use EV/ARR or EV/Revenue instead of P/E
- Rule of 40 helps compare growth vs profitability tradeoff
- Path to profitability matters for cash-burning companies

## Semiconductors

**Representative Companies:** NVDA, AMD, AVGO, INTC, TSM, ASML

| Metric | Description | Good Level |
|--------|-------------|------------|
| **Gross Margin** | Gross profit margin, reflects tech advantage | 50%+ excellent |
| **R&D Ratio** | R&D / Revenue, technology investment | 15-25% appropriate |
| **Inventory Days** | Inventory turnover speed | Varies with cycle |
| **Book-to-Bill** | Orders / Shipments ratio | 1.0+ healthy demand |
| **TAM (Total Addressable Market)** | Total market size | Growth potential |
| **Market Share** | Share in specific chip segment | Track vs competitors |

### Key Considerations
- Cyclical industry - inventory and book-to-bill signal cycle position
- Gross margin indicates competitive moat
- Design wins and customer concentration matter
- Geopolitical risks (especially for TSMC, ASML)
